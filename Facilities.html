Facilities


Wifi

In Patkai Men's Hostel there is 24*7 free high speed internet enabled wifi service available to the use of boarders.

Mess

At the ground floor of Patkai Men's Hostel there is a mess for boarders, which is capable to accommodate 120+ boarders that can be served at once. At a regular of basis mess manager are changed every month. Mess also includes scrub room and purified water source for the drinking water.

Common Room

In the first floor of Patkai Men's Hostel there is a big common room which used by boarders and hostel administration to conduct the hostel meeting and any other small events.

Washroom

In Patkai Men's Hostel there are 6 wings and each wings has separate washroom adjacently with 24x7 water supply. We are also thankful for those sweeper who cleans the washroom and help to provide us clean washroom.
